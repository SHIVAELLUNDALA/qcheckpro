Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how QCheckPro ("the App", "we", "us", "our"), operated by SE Core Business Solutions, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our field audit management application.

1. Who We Are

QCheckPro is a field audit management application built and operated by SE Core Business Solutions, a retail marketing audit company operating across Telangana, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh, India. The App is used by our administrative staff, managers, and freelance field auditors to manage store audits, submissions, and payments.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from users of QCheckPro:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password (encrypted) when you register or are added as a team member.
  • Identity & KYC Documents: For freelance auditors, we may collect identity verification documents (such as Aadhaar) submitted during the registration and approval process.
  • Payment Information: Bank account details, IFSC code, bank name, and UPI ID, used solely for processing payouts to freelance auditors.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your device's GPS location to help identify nearby stores for audit assignment and to record the location of submitted audits.
  • Photographs: Photos taken or uploaded during audit submissions (e.g., store front photos, selfie verification photos) are stored to verify completed audits.
  • Audit & Usage Data: Information related to store audits you complete, including audit responses, timestamps, and submission status.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Assign and track store audits
  • Verify completed audits through photo and location data
  • Process payments owed to freelance auditors
  • Communicate with you regarding audits, approvals, and payments
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the App

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), a cloud infrastructure provider. Access to your data is restricted based on your role within the App (Admin, Manager, or Freelancer/Team Member). We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With our internal administrative and management staff for the purpose of operating the App
  • With our payment processing partners (such as our banking provider) solely to facilitate freelancer payouts
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process

6. Location Permission

QCheckPro requests access to your device's location to help match you with nearby store audits and to record the location at which an audit was submitted. You may decline location access; however, certain features of the App (such as nearby store discovery) may not function correctly without it.

7. Camera and Photo Permission

The App requests access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to capture and upload photographs required for audit submissions.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us using the details below.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us. Freelance auditors may also update their payment and contact details directly within the App.

10. Children's Privacy

QCheckPro is intended for use by adults engaged in field audit work and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
