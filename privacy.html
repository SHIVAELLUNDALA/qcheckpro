Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Overview

QCheck Pro ("the App") is a field audit management application developed and operated by SE Core Business Solutions ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our app on Android or via the web.

By using QCheck Pro, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve the App:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and role (admin, manager, or freelancer) provided during registration.
  • Audit and operational data: Store details, audit records, checklists, scores, and inspection reports you create or upload within the App.
  • Photos and files: Images or documents uploaded as part of field audit submissions, stored securely in Firebase Storage.
  • Payment information: Payout amounts and payment records associated with freelancer audits (no card or banking details are stored by us).
  • Usage data: App activity such as pages visited and actions taken, used to improve the App's performance.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for diagnostic purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and operate the QCheck Pro field audit platform
  • Authenticate users and manage role-based access
  • Store and sync audit records, store data, and reports in real time
  • Process and track freelancer payment records
  • Send operational emails via EmailJS (e.g. audit submissions, notifications)
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

QCheck Pro uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) – Secure user login and session management
  • Cloud Firestore (Google LLC) – Real-time database for storing audit and operational data
  • Firebase Storage (Google LLC) – Secure cloud storage for uploaded photos and files
  • EmailJS – Sending transactional emails for audit submissions and notifications

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access to data is controlled by role-based security rules.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Only authorized users within your organization can access audit data relevant to their role and assigned projects.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Upon deletion, your personal information will be removed from our systems within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (subject to legal obligations)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

QCheck Pro is intended for use by business professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
